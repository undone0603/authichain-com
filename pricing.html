Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a free trial?

Yes! All plans include a 14-day free trial. No credit card required to start. Upgrade or cancel anytime.

What blockchain does AuthiChain use?

AuthiChain uses Polygon (MATIC) for cost-effective, eco-friendly certificate issuance. Enterprise plans can use custom EVM-compatible chains.

Can I integrate with my existing ERP or PIM?

Yes. Our API and webhook system integrates with any ERP, PIM, or e-commerce platform. Full API docs available at /docs.

What happens to certificates if I cancel?

All issued certificates remain permanently on the blockchain. Your customers can always verify authenticity even if you change plans.
